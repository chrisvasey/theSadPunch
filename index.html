The Sad Punch

A short story..

Once upon a time, there was a sad little bowl of punch. He was only some juice and a tiny splash of gin, barely enough for a sip. Day after day he watched other punches rising to levels of greatness he could only dream of, and day after day he was saddened by his own failure.
"If only there was some way I could be better!" He cried.
Suddenly, with a blinding flash of light, a figure appeared before him.
"Who are you?" Asked the punch, quaking in fear. "I am Chris, little punch. I have had visions for your future. Visions of you being the centrepiece of my bed race party and making everybody merry. Will you join me on this journey?" The punch thought this sounded wonderful, but was still upset.
"How will this happen Chris? I am only a little bowl of punch, not worthy of a great party like the one you speak of." At this, Chris smiled. "I have done it once before, little punch. I have called on the bank accounts of my friends and they have answered. I have delved into the depths of their student overdrafts and withdrawn sufficient alcohol funds. I have seen a new year brought in bathed in alcohol and skittles. And I will see it done once more."
And with that glorious statement, Chris turned and broke the fourth wall.
"Now it is your turn, dear readers! Click the button below and give me money for punch."
